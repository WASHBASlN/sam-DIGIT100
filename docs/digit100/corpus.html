Corpus Analysis of the Warriors Superedition books by Erin Hunter.

The auther Erin Hunter is not actually a person, but rather several different people operating under the same pseudonym. I am curious to see if their writing styles vary or not. Of the 14 books I've collected for this analysis, all of them were either written by Kate Cary or Cherith Baldry. Dan Jolley was credited once, alongside Cherith Baldry for the writing of Bramblestar's Storm.

Bluestar's Prophecy, Crookedstar's Promise, Leopardstar's Honor, Moth Flight's Vision, Squirrelflight's Hope, Tallstar's Revenge, and Tigherheart's Shadow were all written by Kate Cary.

Bramblestar's Storm, Crowfeather's Trial, Firestar's Quest, Graystripe's Vow, Hawkwing's Journey, SkyClan's Destiny, and Yellowfang's Secret were written by Cherith Baldry.

All of this information was available to me on the Warrior Cats Fandom Wiki by simply searching for each book by name.

First thing, I went in and simply compared the texts at a very face value. I set the N-gram size to 5, because I feel like that's a good, solid number. Anything less that that was basically the same. Anything more would typically bring up lines of dialogue that are frequently repeated, so this is not an indication of the author's writing style.

I took some of the more commonly occuring phrases and did the cluster search on them. Results below.

While there are some slight differences in their writing styles and word choices it seems, the writing is simple and repetitive enough that a reader likely would not notice the difference. On one had, this makes sense, because they'd probably like to uphold the Erin Hunter persona. But at the same time, there is the background knowledge and context that, historically, the Erins have not been too good at keeping track of the books. One character died 3 different times or something like that because they forgot they already killed him and brought him back.

It was interesting to look at this. I probably would've gone more in-depth if this wasn't supposed to be a quick little assignment and also if AntConc stopped crashing every time I tried to do one little thing. I'm a bit interested to see how similar the writing is in main series books, since the Super Editions are kind of like side books.
